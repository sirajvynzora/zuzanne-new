Meet the Founder

A Journey Stitched with Faith and Love

Zuzanne is a unique brand dedicated to crafting premium christening outfits and ceremonial attire for babies. Inspired by Psalm 92:12–14, Zuzanne symbolizes a plant in the House of God—firmly rooted in faith, blooming fresh and flourishing in the loving presence of Mother Mary.

Every Zuzanne creation reflects the purity, grace, and sacredness of a child's first step into the Christian faith. Combining timeless craftsmanship with elegant design, we create heirloom-quality christening gowns, baptism robes, ceremonial suits, rompers, and accessories that honor this precious occasion.

Each garment is thoughtfully handcrafted using premium fabrics, delicate lace, intricate embroidery, and meticulous attention to detail, ensuring exceptional comfort and lasting beauty. From premature baby gowns to toddler christening outfits for both boys and girls, every piece is designed to become a treasured part of your family's spiritual journey.

For nearly a decade, Zuzanne has been trusted by families to make christenings and other sacred ceremonies truly memorable. More than a clothing brand, Zuzanne represents faith, tradition, and the joy of celebrating life's most meaningful moments.

Teena Juby
Founder & Lead Designer, Zuzanne
